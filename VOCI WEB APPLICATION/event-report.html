Youth Mentorship Program: Empowering Future Leaders

Date: June 15, 2025 | Participants: 100+ Youth | Location: Murang'a Community Hall

The Voice of Change Initiative (VOCI) successfully launched its flagship Youth Mentorship Program on June 2, 2025, at the Murang'a Community Hall. This initiative aims to address youth unemployment, education gaps, and mental health challenges by connecting at-risk youth with experienced mentors from various industries.

The launch event brought together over 100 enthusiastic young participants, VOCI alumni, community leaders, and dedicated volunteers. The programâ€™s objectives include providing personalized guidance, fostering skill development in communication and leadership, and creating pathways to employment and higher education.

Key Outcomes and Impact

Within the first two weeks of the program, significant progress has been observed. Over 80% of registered youth have been successfully paired with mentors, and initial feedback sessions indicate high levels of engagement and satisfaction.

  • **Mentorship Pairings:** 85% of youth successfully matched with mentors.
  • **Skill Development:** Early reports indicate improved communication confidence among participants.
  • **Community Engagement:** Strong collaboration established with local NGOs and businesses for internships.

Challenges and Future Steps

While the initial launch was a success, the program faces challenges in securing long-term funding to scale operations and reach a broader demographic. We are actively seeking new partnerships and grant opportunities to ensure the sustainability and expansion of this vital initiative.

We invite all stakeholders and potential partners to join us in making a lasting impact. Your support can help us transform more lives and build a brighter future for the youth in our communities.
