HOW TO JOIN

Your Journey Starts Here

Becoming a VOCI member is a straightforward process designed to welcome you into our transformative community.

1

Explore & Learn

Familiarize yourself with our mission, vision, and programs. Ensure VOCI aligns with your personal growth and leadership aspirations.

2

Submit Application

Complete our online application form with your details and tell us about your primary areas of interest and motivation to join VOCI.

3

Orientation & Welcome

Attend a virtual or in-person orientation session to meet our team, learn program specifics, and get access to the VOCI Participant Portal.

4

Begin Your Journey

Dive into our transformative modules, engage with mentors, participate in events, and start making your impact as a VOCI member.
